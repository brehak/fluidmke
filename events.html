Events That Stick

Annual Chili Cookout

IMAGE

Our annual chili cookout is one of those “plan ahead” days. Friends, regulars, and first-timers bring their best pots. Come hungry, try a bunch, and stick around—there’s always good heat, good drinks, and a friendly people.

Holiday Parties


When the holidays hit, we go all-in. Expect seasonal cocktails, a dressed-up space, and the kind of crowd that actually wants to celebrate together. It’s festive without being forced—show up whenever, grab a drink, and let the night happen.

Packers Games


Game days at Fluid are easy: show up early, grab a seat, and settle in. We keep the energy up, the pours moving, and the vibe friendly—whether you’re a diehard fan or just here for the company. Wear green if you want, or don’t. Either way, you’re welcome. Touchdown shots to anybody who is interested!

Putt-Putt


Yes— mini golf at the bar. It’s light competition, lots of laughs, and a perfect excuse to bring a group of your best pals. Join the tournament, talk some trash (nicely), and then hang out on the patio after. It’s one of our favorite ways to turn a normal night into something memorable.
