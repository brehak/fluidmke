Fun With A Twist

Where the pours are personal, the vibe is easy, and everyone’s welcome.

Fluid offers more than just the basics—our lineup includes draft and local beers, zero-proof options, classic cocktails, quality spirits, and rotating daily specials. There’s always something worth sipping, no matter what you’re in the mood for.

Something For Everybody

Shots & Specials

We keep it simple: solid drinks at fair prices, with rotating specials throughout the week. Whether you’re stopping in for a post-shift pour or kicking off the night, our bartenders know how to make it right without overcomplicating it.

The Lounge

Our back patio is low-key one of the best spots to hang when the weather hits right. Covered, with heaters and a fire pit when it’s cooler, it’s where people end up settling in for hours. No rush, no pressure—just good drinks and a breeze.

Parties & Events

We throw a few events a year that keep people coming back. The annual chili cookout is one of those “don’t miss it” days—homemade recipes, bragging rights, and bar-wide tasting. Our holiday parties go all-out with decor, cocktails, and community. Not every night is an event, but the ones we do? Worth it.

Good People

No gimmicks—just good people who know what they’re doing. Our bartenders are quick with a pour, quicker with a joke, and know most folks by name. Whether it’s your first time or your fiftieth, you won’t feel like a stranger long.

Drinks & Conversation

We’re not a club. We’re not trying to be. Fluid’s the kind of bar where you can actually hear your friends, meet someone new, or just sit and take it all in. We get a mix—young, older, regulars, visitors—but everyone shows up with the same idea: have a good drink in a no-pressure space.

The Right Soundtrack

We keep the volume comfortable and the playlists smart. Expect everything from pop throwbacks to deep house, depending on the night. Local DJs spin now and then, but we’re not about turning the bar into a club—we’re about setting the tone without killing the conversation.

In The Middle of it All

We’re right in Walker’s Point, surrounded by good food, local businesses, and other queer-owned spots. Whether you’re bar-hopping, grabbing a bite, or just looking for a solid place to land—Fluid’s an easy pick and an easier hang.
